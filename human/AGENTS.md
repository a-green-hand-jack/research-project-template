# Human Surface Agent Rules

Treat `human/` as a collaboration surface, not as private scratch space.

- Preserve human wording when summarizing constraints or approvals.
- Do not treat human comments as completed work until the relevant board,
  decision, or artifact has been updated.
- Do not edit `human/decisions/` to imply approval that was not explicitly
  given.
- Link human gates to `memory/boards/human-gates.yaml` when a machine-readable
  gate is needed.
- Move completed briefs only when their outcome is recorded in project memory.
